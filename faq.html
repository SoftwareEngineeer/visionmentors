FAQs

Vision Mentors offers a diverse range of English language programs to cater to various needs. We have programs tailored for children, teenagers, and adults, ensuring that learners of all ages can benefit from our courses. Additionally, we offer Test Prep classes to help students prepare for standardized English language exams, and we provide special classes designed specifically for international students to enhance their language skills and cultural integration.

Our courses are typically structured with a focus on flexibility to accommodate different schedules. We offer both group and 1:1 classes also online classes, with options for evening and weekend sessions. The duration and frequency of classes depend on the specific program.

The number of classes at Vision Mentors varies depending on the program you choose. We offer flexible scheduling options to accommodate different needs and preferences. Typically, our programs include a series of classes held weekly. For specific details on class frequency and duration, please refer to the program information on our website or contact our admissions team for personalized guidance.

Tuition fees at Vision Mentors vary depending on the program and level. We offer transparent pricing, and you can find specific tuition details on our website. Additionally, we provide information on any available scholarships, financial aid, or payment options to help make education accessible.

Our mentors at Vision Mentors are experienced and dedicated educators with a passion for teaching English. They hold relevant degrees and certifications in English language education. Our team includes native English speakers and proficient non-native speakers, ensuring a well-rounded and immersive learning experience for our students. Additionally, our mentors are committed to providing personalized guidance and support to help students achieve their language learning goals.

Our curriculum is comprehensive and covers essential aspects of English language learning, including grammar, reading comprehension, writing skills, speaking and pronunciation, listening comprehension, and vocabulary building. We tailor our content to suit the needs of each program.

Vision Mentors employs a dynamic teaching approach that includes interactive activities, multimedia resources, and personalized learning plans. We believe in creating engaging and effective learning experiences for our students.

Students are regularly assessed and evaluated throughout their time at Vision Mentors. We use various methods, including quizzes, assignments, and exams, to gauge their progress. Additionally, students receive guidance on how to improve their language skills.

Yes, we offer a range of extracurricular activities to enhance language learning. These activities include conversation clubs, cultural events, and language immersion experiences, allowing students to practice English in real-life scenarios.

We provide students with access to experienced Mentors, Junior Mentors and Teaching Assistants, comprehensive study materials, textbooks, online platforms, and language labs. Our goal is to ensure that students have the resources they need to succeed in their English language learning journey.
