History of Vision Mentors

Vision Mentors Academy was founded with a clear vision and unwavering dedication to providing exceptional English language education and mentorship to learners of all ages. Our journey is marked by significant milestones and a steadfast commitment to empowering students to achieve their academic and professional aspirations.

2022: Inception and Humble Beginnings

  • Vision Mentors Academy was founded in January 2022 by Mr. Charles Kim, an accomplished educator with over 20 years of experience in Test Prep, Admission Consulting, and leadership roles at prestigious educational institutions.
  • We began our journey with just three mentors and eight eager students, offering personalized tutoring services in the comfort of our students' homes.

2022: Growth and Expansion

  • By June 2022, our commitment to excellence led to significant growth. We expanded our team to include four mentors and teaching assistants, and our student cohort grew to twelve.
  • Our services extended beyond homes, and we established a presence at West Point Apartment, creating a dynamic learning environment.

2022: A Year of Milestones

  • By December 2022, we had grown to a team of seven mentors and teaching assistants, guiding a diverse group of twenty-five students.
  • We moved into our West Point W1 office, providing a dedicated space for learning and mentorship.

2023: Expanding Horizons

  • In June 2023, our journey reached a remarkable milestone as we proudly welcomed nine mentors and teaching assistants into our growing family.
  • To accommodate the increasing demand for our programs, we opened a new center at Sunshine City, further expanding our reach and impact.

Throughout our history, Vision Mentors Academy has remained true to its core values of excellence, inclusivity, passion, and community. We have consistently strived to provide the highest quality education and mentorship, fostering a learning environment where students can excel and discover their true potential.

Our commitment to lifelong learning, global perspectives, and innovation continues to drive us forward. As we move into the future, we remain dedicated to inspiring, empowering, and shaping the leaders of tomorrow, one mentorship at a time. Our journey is a testament to our belief in the transformative power of education, and we look forward to many more years of growth, impact, and success.
